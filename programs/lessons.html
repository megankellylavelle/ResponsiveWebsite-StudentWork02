Private Lessons

What? Private one-to-one or small group lessons with an SF Skate Club instructor. Bring a friend and learn together. Have the chance to learn or improve your skateboarding skills.

When? Check calendar for availability!

Where? There are many parks in the Bay Area to choose from. Schedule a park that is near you.

Who? All levels and ages welcome.

Cost? $45hr/student; $30hr/student for a small group lesson.

*Also, ask about weekly drop-in lessons for beginners on Sunday mornings at Balboa skate park with Shawn Connolly from 10-12pm.
