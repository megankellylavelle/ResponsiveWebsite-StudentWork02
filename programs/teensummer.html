Teen Summer Camp

SESSION 1: JUNE 25 - JUNE 29
SESSION 2: JULY 30 - AUGUST 3

What?: A one-week, Monday through Friday skateboard Summer Camp
*Tour and skate Bay Area skateparks and street spots
*Receive small group lessons with a sponsored skateboarder
*Film and edit a video clip
*Fun skate related activities

Where?: All over the Bay Area and SF. We meet at the San Francisco Skate Club Hub at 635A Divisidero St. (between Grove and Hayes) at 12:00pm and leave from there to a skate park. We return to the Hub before 5:00pm for other skateboarding related activities and to go over the day’s accomplishments.

Who?: All kids must be 12-15 years old and registered with San Francisco Skate Club. Fees must be paid, and Contact and Medical Information form completed and mailed to San Francisco Skate Club Hub at 635A Divisidero St., S.F., CA 94117 at least one week before the start of a selected session.

Cost?: Each one-week session is $400 (Cost includes daily transportation to and from skate parks, lessons from a sponsored skateboarder, healthy snacks and water, prizes.)

How do I register?: There are 3 easy ways to register.
1) Register online and pay using Paypal.
2) Download and print forms and send them to us with a check.
3) Call us and we would be glad to assist you.
