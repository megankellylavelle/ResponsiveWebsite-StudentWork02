Club Hub

We here at San Francisco Skate Club are committed to teaching children how to skate. While our focus is on the youth, our efforts are driven by the community. We host many of our youth programs from our Divisadero location, where our Club Hub is open from 10AM-7PM daily. Partnering with professional skaters, local artists, schools, and business, our space is open to the public as a gallery and skate shop. If you are enrolled in a program, or just looking to pick up a new deck or spare parts, SFSC welcomes you.
