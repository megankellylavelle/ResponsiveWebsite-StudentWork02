San Francisco Skate Club strives to provide a safe, positive, and fun environment for youth of diverse backgrounds to pursue their passion or desire to skateboard, meet and form friendships with other young skaters, and learn from experienced skateboarders.

Announcements

Enrollment for the following programs and dates are now open! Hurry, space is limited.

Summer Camp
Tour and skate Bay Area skateparks and receive small group lessons with a sponsored skateboarder. 4 sessions open for children (ages 8-13) and 2 sessions open for teens (ages 12-15)

Saturday Skate
Visit and skate 2 different skate parks every Saturday for four Saturdays; receive small group lessons with a sponsored skateboarder. 2 sessions open for children (ages 8-13)

EduSkate
After School Visit our ClubHub and participate in group activities.
